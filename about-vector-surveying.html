The Story of Vector Surveying

Established in 2015. Vector Surveying was founded on a simple plan. That plan being to take on only the jobs that can be performed inordinately well, and to do those jobs quickly and cost effectively. The land surveying industry is predicated on reputation. The reason for this is because the client or customer may not have the means to judge the quality of the service being provided to them. Instead, the client or customer relies upon the testimony of other customers. We realize this and so we strive to do exceptional work and treat everybody squarely.

Tim Hozalski, P.S.

Tim Hozalski, owner, received a Bachelor's Degree in Surveying and Mapping from The University of Akron in 2010 and became licensed as an Ohio Professional Surveyor in 2015. He takes great pride in his work and is passionate about all things he does. Tim also operates a crop farm with his brother and dad in Henrietta Twp, OH. On the farm his specialty is metal fabrication. In the winter you will find him riding his snowmobile.
